Example Use Cases

In Retail...

Capture what stores are hearing from customers

It's the sales review meeting, and the South Texas district is struggling, but it's not clear why. So you pull up South Texas in the Suggestion Box's dashboard, look at the customer feedback that your the store associates reported yesterday and...

Bingo! A local news story recently cautioned against a material in your product and it is scaring customers.

You respond to the store associates who reported this issue with the information they need to reassure customers.

In School Districts...

Organize community feedback in one place

Your community has a lot of feedback and it is everywhere! From E-mails to phone calls to social media -- it is easy to get overwhelmed. Suggestion Box allows you to organize all of this into one place.

Now, the community can vote on the feedback they think is most important, bringing the issues that are salient to the top.

Moreover, you can respond specifically to community members who are invested in that feedback.

With employees...

Enable your employees to organically identify, prioritize, and craft great ideas for improving your business

You know your employees have a wealth of knowledge about your business, if you could just tap it...

With Suggestion Box it's insanely easy for employees to submit ideas the moment they occur to them.

Then, their peers vote on the ideas in real-time, prioritizing the best ones.

Other employees can add details, list concerns, and submit solutions to reach a consensus proposal with no management intervention needed.

Features

Any Device

Submit feedback, the moment you have it, no matter where you are.
The Suggestion Box works on phones, tablets, computers, and also on Slack

We Moderate

We've found that 99% of content users submit is helpful and productive, but we will moderate for you to catch and remove the other 1%

Innovative Voting

A critical benefit of the Suggestion Box is the ability to prioritize feedback with voting and we're at the cutting edge of extracting the nuances from users' preferences

COLLABORATION

One sentence of feedback is not enough to act on...
The Box's collaboration features enable users to deliver a fleshed out feedback proposal including potential solutions

AUTO RESPONSE

Tired of getting the same questions over and over?
Once you've responded to feedback the first time, Suggestion Box can auto-send the response when users submit similar feedback

CUSTOM REPORTS

Aggregated community data provide great insights
Our reports can show you trending Feedback and common themes in your community.
Furthermore, you can segment the data by the characteristics that are important to you
